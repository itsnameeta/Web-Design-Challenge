Wind Speed

The plot doesn't demonstrate a strong correlation between wind speed and latitude. However, the range of the wind speed is narrower around the equator and it broadens moving either North or South away from a zero-degree latitude. In addition, the range of the wind speed on the Northern Hemisphere is twice broader than the one on the Southern Hemisphere.
